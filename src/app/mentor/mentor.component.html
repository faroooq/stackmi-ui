Can I Become a Mentor?

Essence of Time

Mentees expect a personal touch when they select a mentor. You are required to spend 2 hrs of assistance to each mentee per month.

Passion to Help

Over and above everything, it is that desire to give back that is necessary to be a mentor. You will be required to conduct 1:1 mentorship sessions to inspire and guide them.

Open and Empathetic

Students come from varying backgrounds and naturally have varying personalities. Empathy goes a long way, and as a mentor, one must have loads of it.

Experience Matters

Share your experience (both success and failure) with the mentees so they can learn and reach newer heights in their respective careers. Your degree, however, is not a criterion.
